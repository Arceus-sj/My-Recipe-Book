Dhai Vada

This is a favourite snack of all North Indians known as Dahi Vada. This recipe takes an hour of your time and can be prepared with some easily available ingredients in your kitchen. The recipe of dahi vada is made with lentils and yoghurt, this tangy and flavourful dish is loved by people of all ages. This Dahi vada ingredients contains a melange of spices, chutney, crunchy vada, yoghurt and freshly chopped coriander.

Ingredients (4 Servings)

  • 1 cup moong dal(green gram beans)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 cup hung curd
  • black salt as required
  • 1 handful chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 cup refined oil
  • 2 green chilli
  • 2 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 3 tablespoon tamrind chutney

Procedure

  1. Wash and soak moong dal overnight and grind to a fine paste by adding very little water. The batter should be thick enough to make the vada. Beat the batter very well till light and fluffy. Add green chillies and ginger in it and mix well. Make small balls with it and shape in the form of tikkis
  2. Put a kadhai(flat bottomed pan) over a high flame and heat oil in it. Meanwhile, flatten the top of the ball with wet fingers and drop slowly into the hot oil. Deep fry on medium heat for some time and then fry on low heat till golden brown. Remove these deep-fried vadas into a plate with a kitchen towel. Finish making vadas with the rest of the remaining batter and put them in water as you did for the earlier batch. Now, take a bowl of cold water and let them soak for about 30 minutes.
  3. Prepare sweet curd by mixing hung curd with sugar in a bowl. Now, gently press the soaked vadas between your palms and squeeze out the water and lightly crush them. Place them on a plate and add sweet curd over them. Add tamarind chutney over them and garnish with black salt, roasted cumin seeds and coriander leaves.
